About Me

Hi, I am Melody!

I am currently a first-year Master's student studying Information Science at the University of Michigan, specializing in User Experience Research and Design. As a multidisciplinary UX designer with a unique background in psychology and programming, I design with both empathy to users, as well as conceptual back-end knowledge of the products.

I leverage my professional skills at School of Information through a wide range of graduate courses. For instance, I learned UX research, interaction design, graphic design, agile project management and programming during my first year. I believe interdisciplinary learning enables a UX designer to solve problems with multiple perspectives, thus creating better products with creativity and innovation.
